    Wireless Communication Proposal

    Willem Dekker - 436608 Ruben Smit - 423723

    Peripheral

    Device: NRF52 Will provide a Human Interface Device (0x1812) service with a plane angle (0x2763) characteristic. The value of this characteristic will be set using a potentiometer.

    Central & Broadcaster

    Device: Android phone Will visualize the angle of the peripheral and enable setting the angle of the observer. It will provide a Generic Attribute (0x1801) service with a plane angle (0x2763) characteristic.

    Observer

    Device: FiPy Will observe the broadcaster and change the angle of a servo according to the broadcasted angle of the broadcaster.
